===== Military =====

Military is a much needed resource in the world of tkoc. Military units is needed when going to war, stealing resources or when doing magic. Military units include recruits, attack soldiers, defense soldiers, elite soldiers, thieves and magicians.

==== Humans ====


=== Recruits ===


This is the newly educated human military. Every military unit have to go trough this basic training first. Later on you can teach your military units more skills, and maybe even get them to become thieves or magicians. The human recruits are also nice for exploring new land. This military unit needs about 12 days of training


• Attack Points: 1

• Defence Points: 1

• Gold cost: 200

• Knowledge required: Basic Attack


=== Legions ===

This military is specialized in attacking other provinces. They still got their basic defense skill, but they have become much better at attacking. This military unit needs an additional training time of 18 days after finishing recruits training

• Attack Points: 3

• Defence Points: 1

• Gold cost: 450

• Knowledge required: Basic Attack


=== Pikemen ===


This military is specialized in defending the province. They still got their basic attack skill, but they have become much better at defending. This military needs an additional training time for 18 days after finishing recruits training and you also need some food to train this military

• Attack Points: 1

• Defence Points: 3

• Gold cost: 500

• Food cost: 50

• Knowledge required: none


=== Paladins ===


The paladin is of the elite branch in the human military, and can be used both for attacking and defending. This military is better than specialists at both. This unit needs an additional training time of 24 days after finishing recruits training. To train this unit you need gold, food, and metal


• Attack Points: 5

• Defence Points: 5

• Gold cost: 1500

• Metal cost: 500

• Food cost: 300

• Knowledge required: Military Training

=== Thieves ===


A plain attack is not always the best way to go when you are in war with a province. Sometimes I would be wise to use other resources such as thieves to get an advantage. The thieves will always remember the basic skills of an ordinary fighter and can still be useful in ordinary attacks. This military unit needs an additional training time of 24 days after finishing recruits training.


• Attack Points: 1

• Defence Points: 2

• Gold cost: 800

• Food cost: 80

• Knowledge required: Basic Attack
       

=== Wizards ===


As we all know, magic is a very useful resource. It helps both in times of peace, and in times of war. Since the wizards once where recruits, they still know the basics of fighting and be quite useful in ordinary fights. This military unit needs an additional training time of 24 days after finishing recruits training.


• Attack Points: 1

• Defence Points: 1

• Gold cost: 1000

• Food cost: 100

• Knowledge required: Basic Attack
